How it works

Ground

The ground is made up of rectangular huts, it is entirely bordered by walls. The surface of the ground is not known by the BOT! A BOT occupies one square. One unit of energy occupies one square.

Simulation

A game round consists of a communication sequence between the simulator and a bot. Simulator: executes the energy consumption for a BOT BOT: determines the terrain scan level from 0 to 255 Simulator: scanning takes place BOT : Determines an action (none, move, shoot, shield, hide, ...)

Bot caracteristics

A BOT has only one life. He loses his life if his energy level drops to zero. It is simply disconnected from the server. Each BOT starts with 100 units of energy. A BOT has lost if its energy reserve falls to zero. There is no limit to energy storage There is a loss of at least 1 unit of energy for each turn, even without any detection or action taken.

Item caracteristics

Energy capsules

On the playground, it is possible to find energy capsules. Simply pass over an energy capsule to retrieve its contents. Energy capsules will appear randomly on the field.

Protection

It is possible to activate a protective shield. It will be assigned a certain power level, which will be deducted in energy units for activation. At each turn it will consume 1 unit of energy to maintain its power level. If you take 1 hit, it loses 1 unit of power. If its power drops to zero, it is then inactive.

Cloaks

It's possible to make yourself invisible by activating an occulting field. It will be assigned a certain level of power which will be deducted in units of energy for activation. This power level is the distance at which the occulting field is active.

Move rules

It is possible to move one box in the 4 directions : north, south, east and west. The box must be empty or contain an energy capsule. If the box contains an energy capsule: the energy contained in this capsule is then absorbed. If the box is occupied by a block: loss of 1 in energy If the box is occupied by another Bot, each Bot loses one in energy.

Mods

Deathmatch

12 players divided into 2 teams of six fight on a perfectly symmetrical battlefield. As you can see, strategy will be the key word in this mode of play, where each placement will be decisive in order to gain the upper hand over the opponent.

Energy Chase

Energy chase is playable from 2 to 8 players maximum. The goal is simple, the player who collects the most energy wins the game. Beware of traps that will make you reappear on the appearance square. And to the limited number of energy recoverable on the field. Avoid slipping!
